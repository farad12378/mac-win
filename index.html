Mac vs Windows

Mac Windows

Why People Choose Mac

Macs are known for their clean design, seamless ecosystem, and Unix-based system ideal for developers. macOS also tends to have fewer viruses.

Drawbacks

High cost, limited hardware customizability, and fewer game titles are common complaints.

Why People Choose Windows

Windows offers a wide range of hardware compatibility, strong gaming support, and is widely used in offices worldwide.

Drawbacks

Security vulnerabilities, slower updates, and occasional bloatware are common criticisms.
